Innovation

NFC-Enabled Merch.

Tap your branded product with any smartphone — no app needed — and direct your audience to a URL of your choice. A landing page, a portfolio, a booking link, or a product collection.

Perfect for event swag, business cards, conference merch, and branded gifts that do more than sit on a desk.

Enquire about NFC merch →
How it works

An NFC chip is embedded into your branded product. Tap with any modern smartphone — Android or iPhone — to trigger any URL instantly.

Use cases

Conference badges, tote bags, business cards, packaging inserts, event wristbands — anything that benefits from a digital touchpoint.

Updateable

The URL behind the tap can be updated anytime without reprinting the product — giving your merch a longer useful life.
