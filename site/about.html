My Story

I've been learning and building since 2014. My career spans risk consulting at a Big4 firm — working with clients in healthcare, manufacturing, and financial services — a first venture in wellness living products, a two-year MBA at Babson College in Boston, and the founding of UNfabricated mid-pandemic with no job and no work visa.

I grew up in India watching the men of my family build and scale a business. That observation pushed me to work against the odds and build something of my own.

A COVID-19 lockdown stranded me in Canada when I had to launch my D2C company through a trunk show. That did not stop me from launching the brand and creating a meaningful impact by stitching and donating 800 masks across India.

I've since worked across institutional marketing in the US and Canada, grown UNfabricated, coached founders, emceed community events, and advised family businesses — all simultaneously, all with honesty as the baseline.

Mission-driven. Organic-first. Honest above everything. I'm not the loudest voice in the room — I'm the most consistent one for a specific person who needs real answers, not frameworks.
